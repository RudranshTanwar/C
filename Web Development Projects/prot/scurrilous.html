Scurrilous

Scurrilous

"Scurrilous" is the third studio album by Canadian progressive metal band Protest the Hero, released in 2011. The album showcases the band's signature blend of technical musicianship, thought-provoking lyrics, and progressive metal elements.

Album Themes and Lyrics

Unlike their previous albums, the lyrics for "Scurrilous" were primarily written by lead vocalist Rody Walker. The album delves into more personal themes, with songs discussing personal struggles, social issues, and philosophical musings. "Scurrilous" reflects a more mature and introspective direction for the band.

Critical Reception

"Scurrilous" received critical acclaim for its complex song structures, technical precision, and Walker's more personal approach to lyricism. Fans appreciated the album's maturity, while critics praised the band's growth and continued commitment to pushing the boundaries of progressive metal.
