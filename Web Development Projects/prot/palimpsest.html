Palimpsest

Palimpsest

"Palimpsest" is the fifth studio album by Canadian progressive metal band Protest the Hero, released in 2020. The album was highly anticipated and marked the band’s triumphant return after a long hiatus, diving deep into historical and political themes.

Album Themes and Lyrics

"Palimpsest" tackles heavy themes of American history, politics, and societal change, offering a reflection on the myths and realities of progress. The album's title refers to a manuscript that has been written over but still bears traces of its original text, symbolizing the cyclical nature of history.

Critical Reception

"Palimpsest" was widely praised for its ambitious scope, technical precision, and thought-provoking lyrics. Fans and critics alike lauded the band’s ability to blend intricate progressive metal with powerful storytelling, solidifying the album as one of the band's most mature and relevant works.
