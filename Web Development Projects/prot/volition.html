Volition

Volition

"Volition" is the fourth studio album by Canadian progressive metal band Protest the Hero, released in 2013. The album was funded through a successful crowdfunding campaign and features a blend of aggressive metal, intricate progressive elements, and the signature technicality Protest the Hero is known for.

Album Themes and Musicianship

"Volition" showcases the band’s technical prowess, with songs that range from fast-paced, aggressive riffs to complex, progressive structures. The album touches on themes such as free will, personal empowerment, and political injustice, all conveyed through the powerful lyrics of Rody Walker.

Crowdfunding Success

"Volition" is particularly notable for being funded entirely through an Indiegogo crowdfunding campaign, where fans contributed over $300,000 to support the album's creation. This campaign exemplified the loyalty of Protest the Hero's fanbase and their trust in the band's artistic vision. The success of the campaign allowed the band full creative control over the album.

Guest Musicians

The album features guest contributions, most notably from Chris Adler of Lamb of God, who played drums on the record. Adler's contribution brought an additional layer of technical mastery to the album's rhythmic complexity, enhancing the overall dynamic sound of "Volition."

Critical Reception

"Volition" received widespread critical acclaim, with praise directed towards its innovative composition, technical complexity, and the band's decision to embrace a more DIY approach. Fans and critics alike admired the band's ability to remain true to their progressive metal roots while exploring new musical directions.
