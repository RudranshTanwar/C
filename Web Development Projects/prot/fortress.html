Fortress

Fortress

"Fortress" is the second studio album by Canadian progressive metal band Protest the Hero, released in 2008. The album showcases a mix of technical metal, progressive rock, and deep themes of mythology, history, and power.

Album Themes

The album explores themes of ancient feminine power, mythology, and human history. It touches upon the conflict between tradition and progress, drawing inspiration from various historical and mythological sources.

Critical Reception

"Fortress" received widespread acclaim for its intricate musicianship, conceptual depth, and technical prowess. Many critics highlighted the band's evolution from their debut, "Kezia," while staying true to their progressive roots.
